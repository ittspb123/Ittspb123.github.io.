frequently asked questions

Q: What is the check-in and check-out time?

A: Our standard check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. However, if you require an early check-in or late check-out, please contact the front desk and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Q: Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, breakfast is included in the room rate. We offer a complimentary breakfast buffet every morning from 6:30 AM to 10:00 AM.

Q: Do you have parking available for guests?

Yes, we have a parking garage on-site that is available for guests to use. The parking fee is $20 per day, and space is subject to availability.

Q: Is the hotel pet-friendly?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets in our hotel. However, we can recommend nearby kennels or pet-friendly hotels that may be able to accommodate you and your furry friend

Q: What amenities are available in the guest rooms?

Our guest rooms are equipped with a variety of amenities, including free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, a mini-fridge, a coffee maker, and a safe for your valuables. We also offer complimentary toiletries and plush towels for your comfort.
